Xiamen Shopping Guide: Best for Affordable Perfumes

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is famous for its stunning beaches, colonial architecture, and vibrant shopping scene. While most travelers flock to Zhongshan Road for souvenirs or SM City Mall for high-end brands, few realize that Xiamen is also a hidden paradise for perfume lovers. Whether you're hunting for luxury dupes, niche fragrances, or budget-friendly scents, this guide will lead you to the best spots for affordable perfumes in Xiamen.

Why Xiamen is a Perfume Shopper’s Dream

A Mix of Local and International Brands

Xiamen’s shopping districts offer a unique blend of local perfumers and international brands. From small boutiques crafting traditional Chinese fragrances to stores selling imported designer perfumes at discounted prices, there’s something for every budget.

Tax-Free and Duty-Free Options

Thanks to Xiamen’s status as a major port city, many stores offer tax-free shopping, especially in areas like Gaoqi International Airport’s duty-free section. Travelers can snag high-end perfumes at prices much lower than in Western countries.

Street Markets with Surprising Finds

For those who love a good bargain, Xiamen’s street markets and underground malls are treasure troves. Vendors often sell unbranded but high-quality fragrances inspired by popular luxury scents.

Top Places to Buy Affordable Perfumes in Xiamen

1. Zhongshan Road Pedestrian Street

Best For: Local Perfume Shops and Dupes

Zhongshan Road is Xiamen’s most famous shopping street, lined with historic buildings and modern stores. Hidden among the souvenir stalls are small perfume shops selling dupes of Chanel, Dior, and Jo Malone at a fraction of the price.

  • Must-Visit Store: Aroma Lane – A tiny boutique specializing in custom-blended oils and imitation perfumes.
  • Pro Tip: Bargaining is expected here, so don’t hesitate to negotiate for a better deal.

2. SM City Mall Xiamen

Best For: Discounted Designer Fragrances

One of the largest malls in Fujian, SM City Mall houses Sephora, Watsons, and several beauty outlets where you can find discounted designer perfumes. Seasonal sales (especially during Chinese New Year and Singles’ Day) offer massive markdowns.

  • Best Buys: Look for gift sets—they often include full-sized perfumes with free miniatures.
  • Store Highlight: Sephora Xiamen – Carries mid-range brands like Marc Jacobs and Viktor & Rolf at lower prices than in the U.S.

3. Gaoqi International Airport Duty-Free

Best For: Luxury Perfumes Without the Luxury Price Tag

If you’re flying internationally, don’t miss the duty-free shops at Gaoqi Airport. Prices here are often 20-30% cheaper than retail stores.

  • Top Picks: Tom Ford, Creed, and Byredo are frequently available at competitive rates.
  • Shopping Hack: Check the airport’s pre-order system online to reserve perfumes in advance.

4. Xiamen’s Underground Shopping Malls

Best For: Bargain Hunters and Unique Scents

Places like Lianban Underground Mall are packed with stalls selling unbranded but well-crafted fragrances. These are perfect for travelers who want to experiment with new scents without spending much.

  • What to Expect: Many sellers offer testers, so you can try before buying.
  • Popular Choice: Look for "Xiamen Special" perfumes—locally made scents with floral and oceanic notes.

5. Online Perfume Sellers (Taobao/JD Local Pickup)

Best For: Tech-Savvy Shoppers

If you prefer shopping from your hotel, Xiamen has numerous Taobao and JD sellers who offer same-day delivery. Search for “厦门香水” (Xiamen perfumes) to find local vendors.

  • Trusted Seller: Perfume Palace XM – Known for authentic discounted brands.
  • Caution: Always check reviews to avoid counterfeit products.

How to Spot Fake Perfumes in Xiamen

While Xiamen has great deals, counterfeit perfumes are common in street markets. Here’s how to avoid scams:

  • Check the Packaging: Authentic perfumes have crisp logos and batch codes.
  • Smell Test: Fake perfumes often have a strong alcohol scent at first spray.
  • Price Too Good to Be True? If a €100 perfume is selling for €10, it’s likely fake.

Budget-Friendly Perfume Recommendations

For Women:

  • Lancôme La Vie Est Belle (Dupe) – Found in Zhongshan Road shops for under ¥100.
  • Local Floral Scents – Jasmine and osmanthus-based perfumes are Xiamen specialties.

For Men:

  • Davidoff Cool Water (Discount at SM City Mall) – Often 40% off retail price.
  • Xiamen Ocean Breeze – A fresh, aquatic scent sold in underground malls.

Unisex Picks:

  • Jo Malone Wood Sage & Sea Salt (Duty-Free) – Cheaper than in Europe.
  • Tea-Inspired Scents – Many local brands offer fragrances inspired by Fujian’s famous oolong tea.

Final Shopping Tips

  • Cash is King: Smaller shops and markets prefer cash over cards.
  • Weekday Shopping: Malls are less crowded on weekdays, making for a smoother experience.
  • Ask for Samples: Many stores give free samples if you buy multiple items.

Xiamen’s perfume scene is as diverse as its culture—whether you’re splurging on a duty-free luxury scent or haggling for a dupe in a bustling market, the city offers endless fragrant adventures. Happy shopping!
