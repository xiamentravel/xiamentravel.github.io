Xiamen Shopping Guide: Best for Organic and Natural Products

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is not just famous for its picturesque Gulangyu Island or the serene Nanputuo Temple. Over the years, it has also become a hotspot for health-conscious travelers seeking organic and natural products. Whether you're looking for chemical-free skincare, locally sourced teas, or sustainable fashion, Xiamen offers a variety of options that align with eco-friendly lifestyles. Here’s your ultimate guide to shopping for organic and natural products in Xiamen.

Why Xiamen is a Haven for Organic Shoppers

Xiamen’s lush surroundings and commitment to sustainability make it an ideal place to find high-quality organic goods. The city’s proximity to fertile farmlands and its thriving wellness culture have led to a growing market for natural products. From boutique stores to bustling markets, here’s where you can shop guilt-free.

1. Organic Food Markets

Zhongshan Road Organic Corner

Located near the iconic Zhongshan Pedestrian Street, this hidden gem specializes in fresh, pesticide-free produce. Vendors here sell everything from organic fruits and vegetables to free-range eggs and artisanal honey. Don’t miss the seasonal specialties like organic lychees and locally grown oolong tea leaves.

Xianyue Road Farmers' Market

Open every weekend, this market is a favorite among locals and expats. Farmers from nearby villages bring their harvests, ensuring you get the freshest organic produce. Look out for Fujian’s famous red yeast rice and organic mushrooms, which are staples in traditional Chinese medicine.

2. Natural Skincare and Beauty

Herborist (佰草集) Flagship Store

A pioneer in Chinese herbal skincare, Herborist combines ancient recipes with modern science. Their Xiamen store offers a range of organic face masks, serums, and cleansers made from ingredients like ginseng and green tea. The staff often provides free consultations to help you find the perfect match for your skin type.

L’Occitane en Provence

While not a local brand, L’Occitane’s Xiamen outlet is worth visiting for its natural, ethically sourced products. Their lavender-scented soaps and shea butter hand creams are traveler favorites for their soothing properties.

3. Eco-Friendly Fashion and Textiles

Bamboo Tribe

This boutique champions sustainable fashion with clothing made from bamboo fiber, organic cotton, and hemp. Their lightweight, breathable fabrics are perfect for Xiamen’s humid climate. Plus, their minimalist designs are both stylish and planet-friendly.

Min’s Handicrafts

For those who love handmade goods, Min’s offers beautifully crafted bags and accessories using natural dyes and recycled materials. Each piece tells a story of Fujian’s rich textile heritage.

4. Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Shops

Tongrentang (同仁堂)

A household name in TCM, Tongrentang’s Xiamen branch stocks organic herbs, teas, and supplements. Whether you need goji berries for immunity or chrysanthemum tea for relaxation, their knowledgeable staff can guide you.

Green Valley Organic TCM

Specializing in rare, wild-harvested herbs, Green Valley is a must-visit for wellness enthusiasts. Their organic cordyceps and astragalus root are popular for boosting energy and vitality.

5. Specialty Tea Shops

Wuyi Origin Tea House

Fujian is renowned for its tea culture, and Wuyi Origin offers some of the finest organic oolong and white teas. The serene tasting sessions here are a great way to unwind after a day of exploring.

Tenfu Tea Museum & Shop

Part museum, part retail space, Tenfu showcases the art of tea-making. Their organic Tieguanyin (Iron Goddess of Mercy) is a must-try, with its floral aroma and smooth finish.

6. Zero-Waste and Bulk Stores

The Green House

Xiamen’s first zero-waste store, The Green House, encourages shoppers to bring their own containers. From organic grains to biodegradable cleaning products, this place is a treasure trove for sustainable living.

Eco & More

Another great spot for bulk shopping, Eco & More offers organic spices, nuts, and dried fruits. Their locally made beeswax wraps are a fantastic alternative to plastic cling film.

Tips for Shopping Organic in Xiamen

  • Language Barrier: While many stores have English labels, carrying a translation app can help with ingredient lists.
  • Seasonal Products: Ask vendors about seasonal specialties for the freshest picks.
  • Certifications: Look for labels like China Organic or USDA Organic for guaranteed quality.

Xiamen’s organic shopping scene is a delightful blend of tradition and modernity, offering something for every eco-conscious traveler. Whether you’re stocking up on herbal remedies or hunting for sustainable souvenirs, this city won’t disappoint.
