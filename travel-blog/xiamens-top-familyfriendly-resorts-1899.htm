Xiamen’s Top Family-Friendly Resorts

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is a dream destination for families seeking sun, sand, and unforgettable experiences. With its blend of urban charm and island serenity, the city offers a variety of resorts catering to parents and kids alike. Whether you’re looking for beachfront villas, kid-friendly pools, or cultural adventures, these top family-friendly resorts in Xiamen promise a perfect getaway.

1. The St. Regis Xiamen: Urban Luxury with Family Perks

Why Families Love It

Nestled in the heart of Xiamen’s bustling business district, The St. Regis combines sophistication with family-friendly amenities. The resort’s Butler Service ensures parents can relax while kids enjoy tailored activities like treasure hunts and cookie decorating.

Top Features

  • Family Suites: Spacious rooms with separate living areas and stunning city views.
  • Kids’ Club: Supervised arts, crafts, and games to keep little ones entertained.
  • Indoor Pool: A year-round option for splashing around, rain or shine.

Nearby Attractions

  • Zhongshan Road Pedestrian Street: A foodie paradise with local snacks like sha cha mian (sand tea noodles).
  • Gulangyu Island: A short ferry ride away, perfect for a day trip exploring colonial architecture.

2. InterContinental Xiamen: Lakeside Relaxation & Adventure

Why Families Love It

Overlooking Yundang Lake, this resort blends nature with luxury. The lagoon-style pool and children’s play zone make it a hit for families.

Top Features

  • Family Rooms: Bunk beds and themed decor for younger guests.
  • Bike Rentals: Explore the scenic lakeside trails together.
  • Cooking Classes: Fun sessions where kids learn to make dumplings.

Nearby Attractions

  • Xiamen Botanical Garden: Giant cacti and treetop walks for budding explorers.
  • Hulishan Fortress: A historic site with cannons and coastal views.

3. Sheraton Xiamen Hotel: Beachfront Bliss

Why Families Love It

Located on the golden sands of Huandao Road, Sheraton offers direct beach access and a kids’ splash pool with mini slides.

Top Features

  • Private Beach Cabana: Shaded loungers for parents while kids build sandcastles.
  • Teen Game Lounge: Xbox and VR games to keep older kids engaged.
  • Seafood Buffets: Fresh local catches, including oyster omelets, a Xiamen specialty.

Nearby Attractions

  • Xiamen University: Stroll through lush campuses and the famed Furong Tunnel.
  • Nanputuo Temple: A cultural pitstop with serene gardens.

4. Le Meridien Xiamen: Artsy Escapes for Creative Families

Why Families Love It

This resort doubles as an art gallery, featuring rotating exhibitions. The DIY pottery workshops and family movie nights add a creative twist.

Top Features

  • Art-themed Suites: Rooms adorned with local artists’ works.
  • Rooftop Garden: A quiet escape with panoramic city views.
  • Parent Wellness Packages: Spa credits while kids enjoy supervised playtime.

Nearby Attractions

  • Xiamen Art Museum: Interactive exhibits for all ages.
  • Shapowei Art Zone: Quirky murals and indie cafes.

5. Sofitel Xiamen Wuyuan Bay: French Flair by the Sea

Why Families Love It

A fusion of French elegance and Fujian culture, Sofitel offers kids’ cooking classes (think macarons with a local twist) and a giant chessboard by the pool.

Top Features

  • Family Glamping: Luxury tents with storytelling sessions under the stars.
  • Indoor Playground: A rainy-day lifesaver with ball pits and slides.
  • Parent Date Night: Complimentary babysitting for romantic dinners.

Nearby Attractions

  • Wuyuanwan Wetland Park: Birdwatching and cycling paths.
  • Xiamen Science & Technology Museum: Hands-on experiments for curious minds.

Pro Tips for Booking

  • Off-Peak Visits: Weekdays or non-holiday periods mean fewer crowds.
  • Package Deals: Look for "Stay & Play" bundles including tickets to attractions like Gulangyu Piano Museum.
  • Local Apps: Use Didi (China’s Uber) or Meituan for discounted activity bookings.

From urban luxury to beachside fun, Xiamen’s resorts ensure every family member leaves with a smile. Pack your bags—adventure awaits!
