Xiamen Airport to Xiamen Supermarket – Best Ways to Go

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is a traveler’s paradise with its blend of urban charm and island serenity. Whether you’re here for the famous Gulangyu Island, the bustling Zhongshan Road, or the mouthwatering street food, one thing’s for sure—you’ll need to stock up on snacks or essentials at some point. That’s where Xiamen’s supermarkets come in. But first, you’ve got to get there from the airport.

Getting from Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport to the City

Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport (XMN) is well-connected to the city center, making it easy to reach major supermarkets like Walmart, RT-Mart, or local favorites such as Yonghui Supermarket. Here are the best ways to make the trip.

By Metro: Fast and Affordable

Xiamen’s metro system is efficient, clean, and budget-friendly. Line 1 (Red Line) connects the airport to downtown Xiamen, with stops near major supermarkets.

Steps to Take:
1. From Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport Station, take Line 1 toward Zhenhai Road.
2. If heading to Walmart (Hubin Bei Road), exit at Lianban Station and transfer to a short taxi ride (about 10 minutes).
3. For RT-Mart (SM City Xiamen), stay on Line 1 until Tianhong Station, then walk 5 minutes to the mall.

Pros:
- Cheap (around ¥4-6 per trip).
- Avoids traffic jams.
- Air-conditioned and comfortable.

Cons:
- May require transfers or short walks.

By Taxi: Convenient but Pricier

If you’re carrying heavy luggage or prefer door-to-door service, taxis are a solid choice.

Key Routes:
- To Walmart (Hubin Bei Road): ~25 minutes, ¥50-70.
- To RT-Mart (SM City Xiamen): ~30 minutes, ¥60-80.

Tips:
- Use Didi (China’s Uber) for fixed pricing.
- Avoid unlicensed cabs—stick to the official taxi queue.

By Bus: Budget-Friendly but Slower

Xiamen’s bus network is extensive, though less convenient for tourists with luggage.

Best Routes:
- Bus 37: Connects the airport to Zhongshan Road, near several supermarkets.
- Bus 91: Goes to Wanda Plaza, where you’ll find a Yonghui Supermarket.

Pros:
- Very cheap (¥1-2).
- Scenic views of the city.

Cons:
- Longer travel time.
- Crowded during peak hours.

Top Supermarkets in Xiamen for Travelers

Now that you know how to get there, here are the best supermarkets to visit.

Walmart (Hubin Bei Road)

A reliable choice for international brands and local goods.

Why Go?
- Wide selection of snacks, drinks, and travel essentials.
- Located near tourist areas like Zhongshan Road.

RT-Mart (SM City Xiamen)

One of the largest hypermarkets in Xiamen.

Why Go?
- Great for bulk purchases.
- Adjacent to SM Mall, perfect for shopping and dining.

Yonghui Supermarket

A local chain with fresh produce and regional specialties.

Why Go?
- Authentic Fujian snacks like peanut soup and oyster cakes.
- Often found in malls like Wanda Plaza.

Insider Tips for a Smooth Trip

  1. Avoid Rush Hour: 7:30–9:30 AM and 5:00–7:00 PM can mean heavy traffic.
  2. Cashless Payments: Use Alipay or WeChat Pay—few taxis/buses accept cash.
  3. Google Maps Doesn’t Work: Download Baidu Maps or Amap for navigation.

Whether you choose the metro, taxi, or bus, getting from Xiamen Airport to a supermarket is a breeze. Now go grab those snacks and enjoy your trip!
