How to Spend 5 Perfect Days in Xiamen

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is a dream destination for travelers seeking a mix of urban charm, island vibes, and cultural richness. Whether you're a foodie, history buff, or beach lover, this 5-day itinerary will help you experience the best of Xiamen.

Day 1: Exploring Gulangyu Island

Morning: Ferry Ride & Sunlight Rock

Start your adventure with a short ferry ride to Gulangyu Island, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Arrive early to avoid crowds and head straight to Sunlight Rock (Riguang Yan), the island’s highest point. The panoramic views of Xiamen’s skyline and the ocean are breathtaking.

Afternoon: Shuzhuang Garden & Piano Museum

Stroll through Shuzhuang Garden, a seaside retreat with lush landscapes and hidden pathways. Don’t miss the Piano Museum, showcasing antique pianos that reflect Gulangyu’s musical heritage.

Evening: Longtou Road & Seafood Feast

Wander along Longtou Road, the island’s bustling hub filled with cafes, souvenir shops, and colonial architecture. For dinner, indulge in fresh seafood at a local restaurant—try the oyster omelet or steamed crab.

Day 2: Xiamen’s Urban Highlights

Morning: Nanputuo Temple

Begin at Nanputuo Temple, a serene Buddhist temple at the foot of Wulao Peak. Climb the stairs behind the temple for a scenic hike and city views.

Afternoon: Xiamen University & Zhongshan Road

Visit Xiamen University, one of China’s most beautiful campuses, with its lakes and colonial buildings. Nearby, explore Zhongshan Road, a pedestrian street lined with shops and snack stalls. Sample shacha noodles or peanut soup.

Evening: Zeng Cuo An Village

Head to Zeng Cuo An, a bohemian fishing village turned artsy hotspot. Browse indie boutiques, sip coffee at a beachfront café, and enjoy live music as the sun sets.

Day 3: Day Trip to Tulou (Fujian’s Earthen Buildings)

Full-Day Excursion

Take a guided tour to the Fujian Tulou, UNESCO-listed circular earthen buildings in Yongding County. Visit Chengqi Lou (the "King of Tulou") and Zhencheng Lou, learning about Hakka culture. Stop for a rustic lunch in a local village.

Return to Xiamen

Arrive back by evening and relax at your hotel or explore Hepan Night Market for late-night street food.

Day 4: Coastal Relaxation & Island Hopping

Morning: Hulishan Fortress

Explore Hulishan Fortress, a historic coastal defense site with cannons and ocean views. Walk along the nearby Hulishan Seashore Boardwalk.

Afternoon: Wanshi Botanical Garden

Escape to Wanshi Botanical Garden, a lush oasis with exotic plants, waterfalls, and the iconic Stone Rock Temple.

Evening: Island Hopping to Kinmen (Optional)

For an offbeat experience, take a ferry to Kinmen (Jinmen), a Taiwanese island near Xiamen. Explore its wartime tunnels and traditional villages before returning.

Day 5: Shopping & Leisure

Morning: Overseas Chinese Museum

Dive into Xiamen’s diaspora history at the Overseas Chinese Museum, then shop for souvenirs at SM Mall or Luxi Pedestrian Street.

Afternoon: Yefengzhai & Coffee Culture

Unwind at Yefengzhai, a trendy district with hipster cafes and bookstores. Try Xiamen’s local coffee blends paired with pineapple cakes.

Evening: Sunset at Baicheng Beach

End your trip at Baicheng Beach, watching the sunset over the Taiwan Strait. Reflect on your journey over a farewell dinner at a seaside restaurant.

With its blend of history, nature, and modernity, Xiamen offers an unforgettable escape. Pack your bags and get ready for five perfect days in this coastal paradise!
