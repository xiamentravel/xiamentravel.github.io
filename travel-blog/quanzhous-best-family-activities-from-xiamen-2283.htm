Quanzhou’s Best Family Activities from Xiamen

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Quanzhou, a historic port city in Fujian Province, is a fantastic destination for families traveling from Xiamen. With its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural landscapes, and kid-friendly attractions, Quanzhou offers endless opportunities for memorable adventures. Whether you're exploring ancient temples, strolling through scenic parks, or indulging in local delicacies, there’s something for everyone. Here’s a curated list of the best family activities in Quanzhou that you won’t want to miss.

Exploring Quanzhou’s Cultural Gems

Visit the Kaiyuan Temple

One of Quanzhou’s most iconic landmarks, Kaiyuan Temple, is a must-visit for families. This ancient Buddhist temple, dating back to the Tang Dynasty, features stunning architecture, intricate carvings, and serene courtyards. Kids will love spotting the mythical creatures adorning the temple’s pillars, while parents can appreciate the spiritual ambiance.

Discover the Maritime Museum

Quanzhou was once a major hub of the Maritime Silk Road, and the Maritime Museum brings this history to life. Interactive exhibits, model ships, and artifacts make it an engaging experience for children. The museum’s outdoor section even includes a replica of an ancient trading vessel—perfect for imaginative play!

Walk Through Qingjing Mosque

As one of China’s oldest mosques, Qingjing Mosque offers a unique cultural experience. The blend of Arabic and Chinese architectural styles fascinates visitors of all ages. The surrounding neighborhood is also great for sampling halal snacks like beef dumplings and sesame cakes.

Outdoor Adventures for the Whole Family

Hike at Qingyuan Mountain

For families who love nature, Qingyuan Mountain is a fantastic escape. The trails are well-maintained, and the summit offers breathtaking views of Quanzhou. Along the way, you’ll encounter waterfalls, caves, and even a giant statue of Laozi. Pack a picnic and make a day of it!

Relax at East Lake Park

East Lake Park is a favorite among locals for its scenic beauty and recreational activities. Rent paddle boats, cycle around the lake, or let the kids run free in the playground. The park’s lush greenery and tranquil atmosphere make it a perfect spot for a family outing.

Explore Chongwu Ancient City

A short drive from Quanzhou, Chongwu Ancient City is a hidden gem. The well-preserved stone walls and traditional houses transport visitors back in time. Kids will enjoy climbing the ramparts and pretending to be ancient warriors. Don’t miss the nearby beach for some fun in the sun!

Fun and Educational Experiences

Try a DIY Puppet Show at Quanzhou Puppet Theater

Quanzhou is famous for its string puppetry, and the Quanzhou Puppet Theater offers interactive workshops. Kids can learn how to manipulate the puppets and even put on a mini-show. The colorful costumes and lively performances are sure to captivate young audiences.

Learn Pottery at Dehua Ceramic Workshops

Dehua, known as the "Porcelain Capital," is just a short trip from Quanzhou. Many workshops allow visitors to try their hand at pottery-making. It’s a hands-on activity that sparks creativity—plus, you get to take home a unique souvenir!

Visit the Quanzhou Children’s Library

If you need a quiet break, the Quanzhou Children’s Library is a great stop. With a wide selection of books and interactive learning zones, it’s a wonderful place for kids to unwind and explore new stories.

Delicious Family-Friendly Eats

Savor Quanzhou’s Famous Oyster Omelette

No visit to Quanzhou is complete without trying the oyster omelette (蚵仔煎, pronounced ô-á-jiān). Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, this local favorite is a hit with kids and adults alike. Head to Xunpu Village for the freshest oysters!

Treat the Kids to Peanut Soup

Quanzhou’s peanut soup (花生汤, huāshēng tāng) is a sweet, comforting drink that’s perfect for little ones. Served warm or cold, it’s a delightful way to end a meal.

Explore the Night Market at Zhongshan Road

For a lively evening outing, Zhongshan Road’s night market is packed with street food stalls, games, and souvenirs. Let the kids pick out snacks like grilled squid, candied fruit, or bubble tea while you soak in the vibrant atmosphere.

Easy Day Trips from Xiamen to Quanzhou

Take the High-Speed Train

The high-speed train from Xiamen to Quanzhou takes just 30 minutes, making it an effortless day trip. Once you arrive, the city’s efficient public transport makes getting around a breeze.

Book a Guided Tour

If you prefer a hassle-free experience, consider booking a family-friendly guided tour. Many operators offer itineraries that cover Quanzhou’s top attractions with engaging storytelling tailored for kids.

Combine with a Beach Stop in Hui’an

Extend your trip by visiting Hui’an’s beautiful beaches on the way back to Xiamen. The golden sands and gentle waves are perfect for a relaxing family afternoon.

Quanzhou’s blend of history, nature, and fun activities makes it an ideal destination for families. Whether you’re spending a day or a weekend, these experiences will create lasting memories for both kids and adults. So pack your bags, hop on the train, and get ready for an unforgettable adventure!
