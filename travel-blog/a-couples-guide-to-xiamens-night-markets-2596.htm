A Couple’s Guide to Xiamen’s Night Markets

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is famous for its laid-back vibe, stunning beaches, and—most importantly—its vibrant night markets. For couples looking to explore the city after dark, Xiamen’s night markets offer a perfect blend of romance, adventure, and mouthwatering street food. Whether you’re holding hands while browsing handmade crafts or sharing a plate of sizzling oysters, these markets are a must-visit. Here’s your ultimate guide to making the most of Xiamen’s nightlife together.

Why Xiamen’s Night Markets Are Perfect for Couples

A Mix of Romance and Excitement

There’s something undeniably romantic about wandering through bustling markets under twinkling lights. The energy is contagious, and the variety of sights, sounds, and smells creates an unforgettable experience.

Affordable Date Night

Unlike fancy restaurants, night markets let you sample a wide range of local delicacies without breaking the bank. Plus, the casual atmosphere makes it easy to relax and enjoy each other’s company.

Unique Souvenirs

From handmade jewelry to quirky trinkets, Xiamen’s night markets are treasure troves for couples looking to bring home a special memento.

Top Night Markets for Couples in Xiamen

Zhongshan Road Night Market

What Makes It Special

Zhongshan Road is Xiamen’s most famous pedestrian street, transforming into a lively night market as the sun sets. The mix of colonial architecture and modern neon lights creates a dreamy backdrop for couples.

Must-Try Foods

  • Oyster Omelette (蚵仔煎, pronounced ô-á-jiān) – A crispy, savory delight.
  • Peanut Soup (花生汤, huāshēng tāng) – Sweet and comforting.
  • Fried Squid Balls – Crispy on the outside, tender inside.

Best Couple Activity

Stroll hand-in-hand while stopping at street performers’ shows or posing for photos against the colorful backdrops.

Shapowei Art Zone Night Market

What Makes It Special

For artsy couples, Shapowei is a hipster paradise. Once an old fishing village, it’s now filled with indie boutiques, graffiti walls, and cozy cafés.

Must-Try Foods

  • Grilled Scallops with Garlic – Juicy and fragrant.
  • Mango Shaved Ice – A refreshing treat.
  • Stuffed Tofu Puffs – Crispy bites filled with savory goodness.

Best Couple Activity

Browse local art galleries or sip coffee at a rooftop café overlooking the harbor.

Gulangyu Night Market (Pier Area)

What Makes It Special

Though Gulangyu itself is quieter at night, the nearby pier area buzzes with food stalls and live music. The sea breeze adds a touch of romance.

Must-Try Foods

  • Seafood Hotpot – Fresh catches cooked right in front of you.
  • Taro Balls (芋圆, yù yuán) – Chewy and sweet.
  • Coconut Pudding – A light, tropical dessert.

Best Couple Activity

Take a short ferry ride to Gulangyu and enjoy the island’s serene night views before heading back for snacks.

Tips for Couples Visiting Xiamen’s Night Markets

Go Early to Avoid Crowds

Popular markets like Zhongshan Road get packed after 8 PM. Arriving around 6:30 PM ensures you get the best food without long waits.

Bring Small Bills

Many vendors prefer cash, especially for small purchases. Having exact change speeds things up.

Wear Comfortable Shoes

You’ll be walking a lot, so ditch the heels for sneakers or sandals.

Try Sharing Dishes

Portions are often generous, so splitting food lets you taste more without overeating.

Learn a Few Mandarin Phrases

While many vendors understand basic English, knowing phrases like "Zhège duōshǎo qián?" (How much is this?) can be helpful.

Hidden Gems for Couples

Xiamen University Night Market

Located near one of China’s most beautiful campuses, this market is popular with students, meaning great food at budget prices. Try the jianbing (savory crepes) and bubble tea.

Hulishan Fortress Night Market

A lesser-known spot with stunning sea views. Perfect for couples who want a quieter, more intimate experience.

Nanputuo Temple Night Market

If you’re looking for vegetarian options, this market near the famous temple offers delicious Buddhist-style snacks.

Final Thoughts

Xiamen’s night markets are more than just places to eat—they’re experiences that bring couples closer. Whether you’re foodies, art lovers, or just enjoy a good stroll, these markets have something for every duo. So grab your partner’s hand, follow the neon lights, and dive into the magic of Xiamen after dark.
