Best Restaurants with NYE Specials in Xiamen

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is not just famous for its stunning beaches and colonial architecture but also for its vibrant culinary scene. As New Year’s Eve (NYE) approaches, the city’s top restaurants roll out exclusive menus, live entertainment, and breathtaking views to make the celebration unforgettable. Whether you’re a local or a traveler ringing in the new year in this picturesque city, here’s a curated list of the best restaurants offering NYE specials in Xiamen.

Fine Dining with a View

1. Gulangyu Seafood Palace

Perched on the iconic Gulangyu Island, this upscale seafood restaurant offers a NYE feast with panoramic ocean views. Their special menu includes fresh lobster, abalone, and locally sourced fish paired with premium champagne. Live jazz music and a midnight fireworks display make it a top pick for a luxurious celebration.

2. Sky Lounge at The LUX* Xiamen

For those who prefer a rooftop experience, Sky Lounge delivers with its 360-degree cityscape views. Their NYE package features a gourmet tasting menu, signature cocktails, and a DJ set to keep the party going until dawn. Reservations fill up fast, so book early!

Authentic Fujian Cuisine

3. Min Wei Xuan

A must-visit for foodies craving authentic Fujian flavors, Min Wei Xuan’s NYE special includes classics like Buddha Jumps Over the Wall (佛跳墙, Fó tiào qiáng) and Oyster Omelette (蚵仔煎, Kè zǎi jiān). The rustic-chic ambiance and traditional live music create a cozy yet festive atmosphere.

4. Shapowei Teahouse & Eatery

Located in the artsy Shapowei district, this spot blends Fujian tea culture with gourmet dining. Their NYE menu highlights tea-infused dishes like Tieguanyin Smoked Duck and a dessert platter featuring local sweets. Pair your meal with a curated tea tasting session.

International Flavors

5. La Villa Negroni

If Italian cuisine is your NYE craving, La Villa Negroni’s multi-course dinner with truffle pasta, osso buco, and tiramisu won’t disappoint. The intimate setting and live opera performances add a touch of European elegance.

6. The Brewery Xiamen

Beer lovers rejoice! This German-style brewpub hosts a lively NYE bash with unlimited craft beer, pretzels, and bratwurst. The countdown includes a tapping of their special "Midnight Brew" at 12 AM.

Romantic Spots for Couples

7. The Sunset Grill

Overlooking Hulishan Fortress, this beachfront grill offers a private NYE dinner under the stars. Think surf-and-turf platters, rose petals, and a personalized dessert with fireworks as your backdrop.

8. Le Café Français

A quaint French bistro in Zhongshan Road, Le Café Français charms with its NYE menu dégustation and vintage champagne. The candlelit courtyard is perfect for a quiet, romantic toast.

Family-Friendly Options

9. Happy Lamb Hot Pot

Warm up with a communal hot pot experience at this popular chain. Their NYE family set includes a variety of broths, premium meats, and a DIY dessert bar. Kids get party favors and a balloon artist to keep them entertained.

10. Pizza Marzano

For a casual yet festive vibe, Pizza Marzano’s NYE party features build-your-own pizzas, a gelato station, and a magician show. It’s a hit with families and groups of friends.

Late-Night Bites & After-Parties

11. Night Market Street Stalls

For a more local NYE experience, hit the night markets near Zhongshan Road. Try satay noodles (沙茶面, Shā chá miàn) or fried oyster pancakes while soaking in the lively street performances.

12. Club Viva

Cap off the night at Xiamen’s hottest club, where the NYE bash includes international DJs, confetti showers, and VIP bottle service. Dress to impress!

Pro Tips for NYE in Xiamen

  • Book Early: Top restaurants often require deposits or sell out weeks in advance.
  • Dress Code: Upscale venues may enforce smart casual or formal attire.
  • Transport: Taxis are scarce post-midnight; consider booking a Didi in advance.
  • Weather: December evenings can be chilly—bring a light jacket for outdoor venues.

From lavish seafood feasts to cozy teahouse gatherings, Xiamen’s NYE dining scene caters to every taste and budget. Cheers to a delicious start to the new year!
