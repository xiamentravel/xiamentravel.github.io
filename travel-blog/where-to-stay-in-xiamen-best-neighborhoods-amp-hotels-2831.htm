Where to Stay in Xiamen: Best Neighborhoods & Hotels

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is one of China’s most charming destinations. Known for its laid-back island vibes, colonial architecture, and vibrant food scene, it’s a must-visit for travelers seeking a mix of culture and relaxation. But with so many neighborhoods to choose from, where should you stay? Here’s a breakdown of the best areas and top hotels to consider for your trip.

Gulangyu Island: The Quaint Getaway

Why Stay Here?

Gulangyu is a car-free island just a short ferry ride from downtown Xiamen. Famous for its European-style villas, piano museums, and winding alleys, it’s perfect for travelers who love history and a slower pace.

Top Hotels on Gulangyu

  • Lanyue Hotel – A boutique hotel in a restored colonial building with sea views.
  • Gulangyu Island Hotel – A historic property with lush gardens and vintage charm.
  • NAYA Hotel – A trendy, artsy stay with modern comforts.

Things to Do Nearby

  • Explore Shuzhuang Garden and its famous Piano Museum.
  • Climb Sunlight Rock for panoramic views.
  • Stroll along Longtou Road for snacks and souvenirs.

Zhongshan Road: The Heart of Xiamen

Why Stay Here?

Zhongshan Road is Xiamen’s bustling pedestrian street, packed with shops, street food, and nightlife. It’s ideal for travelers who want to be in the center of the action.

Best Hotels Near Zhongshan Road

  • Swiss Grand Xiamen – A luxury option with easy access to shopping.
  • Pan Pacific Xiamen – Modern and upscale, great for business and leisure.
  • Ibis Styles Xiamen Zhongshan Road – Budget-friendly with a fun design.

What to Explore Nearby

  • Try local snacks like sha cha noodles and oyster omelets.
  • Visit Nanputuo Temple, a serene Buddhist site.
  • Walk to Xiamen University and its scenic campus.

Hulishan Fortress & Beach Area: For Ocean Lovers

Why Stay Here?

If you want a mix of history and beach relaxation, this area near Hulishan Fortress is perfect. You’ll find coastal walks, sea views, and fewer crowds.

Top Hotels in the Area

  • The Westin Xiamen – A high-end stay with a private beach.
  • Xiamen International Seaside Hotel – Mid-range with great ocean views.
  • Seaview Resort Xiamen – Affordable and steps from the sand.

Must-Do Activities

  • Tour Hulishan Fortress, home to historic cannons.
  • Relax at Baicheng Beach, a quieter alternative to popular spots.
  • Walk along Huandao Road, Xiamen’s scenic coastal route.

Xiamen University & Nanputuo Area: Culture & Nature

Why Stay Here?

This area blends academia, nature, and spirituality. It’s great for those who enjoy parks, temples, and a youthful atmosphere.

Best Hotels Nearby

  • DoubleTree by Hilton Xiamen – Comfortable and close to the university.
  • Xiamen Haiyue Hotel – A budget pick with good amenities.
  • Lujiang Harborview Hotel – Classic and well-located.

Top Attractions

  • Wander through Xiamen University’s beautiful campus.
  • Hike up Wanshi Botanical Garden for greenery and views.
  • Visit Nanputuo Temple for a peaceful retreat.

Jimei District: A Hidden Gem

Why Stay Here?

Jimei is less touristy but rich in culture, thanks to its ties to overseas Chinese heritage and the famous Jimei School Village.

Recommended Hotels

  • Atour Hotel Xiamen Jimei – Sleek and affordable.
  • Xiamen Jimei Holiday Inn – Reliable and comfortable.
  • Jimei Grand Hotel – A solid mid-range choice.

What to See & Do

  • Explore Jimei School Village, an architectural marvel.
  • Visit Turtle Garden, a scenic lakeside park.
  • Check out Chen Jiageng Memorial Hall, honoring a prominent philanthropist.

Wuyuanwan & Xiamen North: Modern & Convenient

Why Stay Here?

If you’re traveling for business or need quick access to the high-speed rail, these newer districts offer modern comforts.

Top Hotel Picks

  • Sheraton Xiamen Hotel – Upscale with great amenities.
  • Hilton Xiamen – A reliable luxury option.
  • Hyatt Place Xiamen Wuyuanwan – Great for short stays.

Nearby Highlights

  • Walk around Wuyuanwan Park for waterfront views.
  • Visit Xiamen Science & Technology Museum for interactive fun.
  • Take a short trip to Tianzhu Mountain for hiking.

With so many great neighborhoods and hotels, Xiamen offers something for every traveler. Whether you prefer historic charm, beachside relaxation, or urban energy, you’ll find the perfect spot to call home during your stay.
