Xiamen’s Finest: A Curated List of High-End Hotels

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in China’s Fujian province, is a dream destination for travelers seeking a mix of urban sophistication and island charm. Known for its lush greenery, colonial architecture, and vibrant food scene, the city also boasts an impressive lineup of luxury hotels that cater to discerning guests. Whether you’re here for business, leisure, or a romantic getaway, these high-end accommodations promise an unforgettable stay.

The Pinnacle of Luxury: Xiamen’s Top 5-Star Hotels

1. The LUX* Xiamen

Perched along the scenic Huandao Road, The LUX* Xiamen redefines opulence with its sleek design and panoramic ocean views. This hotel is a favorite among celebrities and business elites, offering private beach access, a world-class spa, and gourmet dining curated by Michelin-starred chefs.

Why Stay Here?

  • Exclusive Beachfront Location: Wake up to the sound of waves and enjoy private cabana service.
  • Signature Spa Treatments: Indulge in bespoke therapies using local ingredients like tea and sea salt.
  • Gourmet Dining: The on-site restaurant, Azure, serves fusion cuisine with a focus on fresh seafood.

2. The St. Regis Xiamen

Situated in the heart of the city’s financial district, The St. Regis Xiamen combines timeless elegance with modern convenience. Known for its legendary butler service, this hotel is perfect for those who appreciate personalized attention.

Highlights:

  • Butler Service: From unpacking your luggage to arranging private tours, every need is anticipated.
  • Infinity Pool: A rooftop oasis with stunning skyline views.
  • Afternoon Tea Ritual: A must-try experience in the lavish lobby lounge.

Boutique Luxury: Unique Stays in Xiamen

3. Amoy Hotel by Rhombus

Housed in a beautifully restored colonial building, the Amoy Hotel by Rhombus offers a blend of history and contemporary comfort. Located near Zhongshan Road, it’s ideal for travelers who want to explore Xiamen’s cultural hotspots.

What Makes It Special?

  • Heritage Charm: Original Art Deco details paired with modern amenities.
  • Central Location: Steps away from shopping, dining, and Gulangyu Island ferries.
  • Intimate Atmosphere: Only 30 rooms, ensuring a quiet and exclusive experience.

4. Seven Stars Bay Retreat

For those seeking serenity, Seven Stars Bay Retreat is a hidden gem nestled in a secluded cove. This eco-luxury resort emphasizes sustainability without compromising on comfort.

Eco-Conscious Luxury:

  • Solar-Powered Villas: Designed to minimize environmental impact.
  • Private Hiking Trails: Explore Xiamen’s untouched natural beauty.
  • Farm-to-Table Dining: Ingredients sourced from the resort’s organic garden.

Urban Escapes: High-End Business Hotels

5. Conrad Xiamen

Overlooking Yundang Lake, Conrad Xiamen is a haven for business travelers and leisure seekers alike. Its proximity to the convention center and upscale malls makes it a practical yet luxurious choice.

Business-Friendly Perks:

  • State-of-the-Art Meeting Spaces: Equipped with the latest technology.
  • Executive Lounge: Complimentary breakfast and evening cocktails for elite guests.
  • 24/7 Fitness Center: Stay on top of your workout routine.

6. InterContinental Xiamen

With its striking architecture and prime location near Wuyuanwan, the InterContinental Xiamen is a standout for both business and pleasure.

Standout Features:

  • Sky Lobby: Check-in on the 38th floor with breathtaking views.
  • Champagne Brunch: A weekend favorite among locals and visitors.
  • Kids’ Club: Family-friendly amenities for traveling parents.

Insider Tips for Booking Your Stay

  • Best Time to Visit: Spring (March-May) and autumn (October-November) offer pleasant weather and fewer crowds.
  • Local Experiences: Don’t miss a sunset stroll along Hulishan Fortress or a food tour of Zhongshan Road’s street eats.
  • Loyalty Programs: Many of these hotels are part of global chains—redeem points for upgrades or free nights.

Whether you’re drawn to Xiamen for its cultural heritage, culinary delights, or scenic beauty, these high-end hotels ensure your stay is as memorable as the destination itself. From beachfront villas to urban skyscrapers, each property offers a unique way to experience the city’s finest.
