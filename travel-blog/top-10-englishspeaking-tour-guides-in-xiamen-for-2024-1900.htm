Top 10 English-Speaking Tour Guides in Xiamen for 2024

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is a dream destination for travelers seeking a mix of urban charm and island serenity. With its stunning beaches, rich history, and vibrant food scene, it’s no wonder tourists flock here year-round. But to truly unlock the city’s secrets, you’ll need an expert guide—especially one who speaks fluent English. Here’s our curated list of the Top 10 English-Speaking Tour Guides in Xiamen for 2024, each offering unique insights and unforgettable experiences.

1. Daniel Chen – The History Buff

Why He Stands Out

Daniel’s deep knowledge of Xiamen’s colonial past and maritime heritage makes him a favorite among culture enthusiasts. His tours often include hidden alleys in Gulangyu Island and lesser-known stories about the European-style architecture.

Signature Tour

"Gulangyu Uncovered" – A deep dive into the island’s UNESCO-listed sites, with stops at the Piano Museum and Sunlight Rock.

2. Lily Zhang – The Foodie Specialist

Why She Stands Out

Lily’s passion for Fujian cuisine is contagious. She takes guests beyond touristy spots to authentic street food stalls and family-run eateries.

Must-Try Experience

"Night Market Crawl" – Sample oyster omelets, peanut soup, and taro dumplings while learning about local culinary traditions.

3. David Wang – The Adventure Guide

Why He Stands Out

For thrill-seekers, David organizes hiking trips to Xiamen’s scenic spots like Wanshi Botanical Garden and Hulishan Fortress.

Top Pick

"Sunrise Hike to Xiamen’s Highest Peak" – A morning trek with panoramic views of the city and coastline.

4. Sophia Lin – The Photography Pro

Why She Stands Out

Sophia helps travelers capture Instagram-worthy shots at Xiamen’s most photogenic locations, from Zhongshan Road’s neon lights to the tranquil beaches of Huandao Road.

Workshop Highlight

"Golden Hour at Baicheng Beach" – Tips on framing the perfect sunset shot with the iconic "I Love Xiamen" sign.

5. Kevin Wu – The Family-Friendly Guide

Why He Stands Out

Kevin specializes in kid-friendly itineraries, including interactive activities like seashell collecting and kite flying at Xiamen’s parks.

Family Favorite

"Gulangyu Treasure Hunt" – A fun, educational game exploring the island’s history with prizes for young explorers.

6. Eva Zhao – The Luxury Travel Curator

Why She Stands Out

Eva caters to high-end travelers, arranging private yacht tours, VIP access to Nanputuo Temple, and bespoke shopping experiences.

Exclusive Offer

"Five-Star Island Escape" – A day of luxury on Gulangyu, complete with a gourmet lunch at a cliffside restaurant.

7. Tom Li – The Eco-Tour Advocate

Why He Stands Out

Tom focuses on sustainable tourism, promoting eco-friendly practices and visits to Xiamen’s green spaces like Yundang Lake.

Eco-Tour Idea

"Plastic-Free Beach Cleanup" – A rewarding combo of sightseeing and environmental activism.

8. Grace Huang – The Art & Culture Expert

Why She Stands Out

Grace connects travelers with Xiamen’s contemporary art scene, from galleries in the Red Town Cultural District to live Minnan opera performances.

Cultural Deep Dive

"Tea Ceremony & Calligraphy Workshop" – A hands-on introduction to Fujian’s artistic traditions.

9. Jack Liu – The Business Traveler’s Ally

Why He Stands Out

Jack helps corporate visitors navigate Xiamen’s business hubs while squeezing in leisure—think quick temple visits between meetings.

Efficient Itinerary

"High-Speed City Tour" – A half-day whirlwind of top landmarks tailored for tight schedules.

10. Mia Tan – The Solo Traveler’s Best Friend

Why She Stands Out

Mia creates safe, social tours for solo adventurers, including group bike rides along Huandao Road and hostel meetups.

Solo Traveler Perk

"Backpackers’ Pub Crawl" – A night of bar-hopping with fellow globetrotters.

Booking Tips for 2024

  • Plan Ahead: Peak seasons (October holidays, summer) require early reservations.
  • Check Reviews: Look for guides with recent 5-star ratings on TripAdvisor or Klook.
  • Customize: Many guides offer personalized tours—don’t hesitate to ask!

Whether you’re a history lover, food fanatic, or adrenaline junkie, Xiamen’s top English-speaking guides are ready to make your 2024 trip extraordinary.
