Xiamen Airport to Xiamen Hospital – Best Transport Routes

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is a top-tier tourist destination known for its blend of modernity and tradition. Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or medical tourism, navigating from Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport (XMN) to Xiamen Hospital efficiently is crucial. This guide explores the best transport routes, insider tips, and scenic detours to make your journey seamless.

Why Xiamen Hospital?

Xiamen boasts world-class healthcare facilities, attracting medical tourists seeking specialized treatments. Xiamen Hospital (also known as Xiamen University Affiliated Hospital or Zhongshan Hospital) is a leading institution with cutting-edge technology and multilingual staff. Whether you're here for a routine check-up or advanced procedures, knowing the fastest and most comfortable transport options is key.

Top Transport Options from Xiamen Airport to Xiamen Hospital

1. Airport Shuttle Bus – Budget-Friendly & Direct

For travelers prioritizing affordability, the airport shuttle bus is a solid choice.

  • Route: Take Bus No. 37 or Bus No. 91 from the airport terminal.
  • Duration: ~45 minutes (depending on traffic).
  • Cost: Only ¥2-5 (less than $1).
  • Pros:
    • Direct route with minimal stops.
    • Air-conditioned buses for comfort.
  • Cons:
    • Limited luggage space.
    • Slower during peak hours.

Pro Tip: If you’re staying near Hulishan Fortress, hop off early and enjoy a quick historical detour!

2. Taxi or Ride-Hailing – Fast & Convenient

For those with tight schedules or heavy luggage, taxis or ride-hailing apps like Didi are ideal.

  • Duration: ~25 minutes (non-rush hour).
  • Cost: ¥50-80 (~$7-12).
  • Pros:
    • Door-to-door service.
    • Available 24/7.
  • Cons:
    • Slightly pricier than public transport.
    • Possible language barrier (have your destination written in Chinese).

Scenic Route Suggestion: Ask the driver to take the Island Ring Road for breathtaking coastal views!

3. Metro (Subway) – Modern & Efficient

Xiamen’s metro system is clean, fast, and expanding.

  • Route:
    • Take Metro Line 1 from Gaoqi Airport Station to Lianban Station.
    • Transfer to Metro Line 2 and alight at Zhongshan Hospital Station.
  • Duration: ~35 minutes.
  • Cost: ¥4-6 (~$0.60-1).
  • Pros:
    • Avoids traffic congestion.
    • English announcements and signage.
  • Cons:
    • Requires a short walk (~10 mins) to the hospital.

Hidden Gem: Stop at Zhongshan Road Pedestrian Street for local snacks before heading to the hospital!

4. Private Car Rental – Flexibility & Comfort

If you prefer autonomy, renting a car is an option (though not recommended for short stays due to parking challenges).

  • Duration: ~20-30 minutes.
  • Cost: ¥200-400/day (~$30-60).
  • Pros:
    • Freedom to explore Xiamen at your pace.
    • Ideal for group travelers.
  • Cons:
    • Parking near hospitals can be tricky.
    • Requires an international driver’s permit.

Best Time to Travel

To avoid delays:
- Peak Hours (7:30-9:30 AM & 5:00-7:00 PM): Opt for the metro.
- Off-Peak: Taxis or buses are quicker.

Nearby Attractions to Combine with Your Trip

Since Xiamen Hospital is centrally located, consider adding these spots to your itinerary:

Nanputuo Temple

A serene Buddhist temple just 15 minutes away—perfect for pre- or post-appointment relaxation.

Xiamen University

One of China’s most beautiful campuses, featuring lush gardens and colonial architecture.

Gulangyu Island

A UNESCO World Heritage site, accessible via a short ferry ride.

Final Tips for a Smooth Journey

  • Language Help: Download Google Translate (offline Chinese pack) or Pleco for quick translations.
  • Payment: Have Alipay or WeChat Pay set up for cashless transactions.
  • Emergency: Save the hospital’s contact info (+86 592 213 2222) and your embassy’s details.

Whether you're a medical tourist or a traveler assisting a companion, Xiamen’s efficient transport network ensures a stress-free trip from the airport to the hospital. Safe travels!
