Xiamen’s Best Places to Try Fresh Seafood BBQ

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Xiamen, a coastal gem in Fujian Province, is a paradise for seafood lovers. With its bustling night markets, open-air grills, and waterfront restaurants, the city offers an unforgettable seafood BBQ experience. Whether you’re a traveler craving authentic flavors or a local looking for the freshest catch, here’s your ultimate guide to the best spots for seafood BBQ in Xiamen.

The Allure of Xiamen’s Seafood BBQ Scene

Xiamen’s seafood BBQ culture is deeply rooted in its coastal lifestyle. The city’s proximity to the Taiwan Strait ensures a steady supply of fresh seafood, from succulent prawns to tender squid. What sets Xiamen apart is its unique blend of Fujianese flavors—think garlic, chili, and a hint of sweetness—paired with smoky, char-grilled perfection.

Why Seafood BBQ is a Must-Try in Xiamen

  • Freshness Guaranteed: Many restaurants source their seafood directly from local fishermen.
  • Affordable Luxury: Unlike high-end dining, seafood BBQ is often wallet-friendly.
  • Vibrant Atmosphere: Night markets and beachside grills create a lively, communal vibe.

Top Spots for Seafood BBQ in Xiamen

1. Zeng Cuo An Seafood Street

Nestled near the famous Zeng Cuo An Beach, this street is a hotspot for seafood enthusiasts. Stalls line the narrow alleyways, offering everything from grilled oysters to spicy clams.

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Grilled Scallops with Garlic: Plump scallops topped with fragrant garlic and vermicelli.
  • BBQ Squid: Chewy yet tender, brushed with a sweet-spicy glaze.
  • Prawn Skewers: Juicy prawns marinated in a secret soy-based sauce.

Pro Tip: Visit around sunset for a breezy, less crowded experience.

2. Hepu Seafood Market

For a DIY seafood BBQ adventure, head to Hepu Seafood Market. Pick your live catch—crabs, lobsters, or fish—and have it grilled on the spot.

Why It Stands Out:

  • Interactive Dining: Choose your seafood and watch it being prepared.
  • Local Vibe: Rub shoulders with Xiamen residents who swear by this spot.

3. Shapowei Art Zone

A trendy area blending art and cuisine, Shapowei offers chic seafood BBQ joints with a creative twist. Think grilled seafood platters paired with craft beer.

Highlights:

  • Artisan Flavors: Fusion dishes like wasabi-infused grilled fish.
  • Instagram-Worthy Ambiance: Graffiti walls and cozy lantern-lit tables.

4. Gulangyu Night Market

While Gulangyu is famous for its colonial architecture, its night market is a hidden gem for seafood BBQ. Wander the cobblestone streets and follow the smoky aroma.

Don’t Miss:

  • Grilled Razor Clams: Topped with minced pork and herbs.
  • Stuffed Crab Shells: A creamy, cheesy delight.

Insider Tips for the Best Seafood BBQ Experience

Timing Matters

  • Early Evening: Avoid the dinner rush (7–8 PM) for quicker service.
  • Weekdays: Weekends can get overwhelmingly crowded.

How to Spot Fresh Seafood

  • Clear Eyes: Fish eyes should be bright, not cloudy.
  • Firm Texture: Press the flesh—it should spring back.
  • Ocean Smell: Fresh seafood smells like the sea, not ammonia.

Pairing Suggestions

  • Local Beers: Try Xiamen Beer or Snow Beer for a crisp contrast.
  • Oolong Tea: Cuts through the richness of grilled flavors.

Beyond BBQ: Other Seafood Delicacies in Xiamen

While BBQ steals the spotlight, don’t miss these seafood dishes:
- Sha Cha Noodles: Wheat noodles with a savory seafood-based sauce.
- Oyster Omelette: A crispy, eggy delight with plump oysters.

Sustainable Seafood Practices in Xiamen

Many vendors now prioritize sustainability. Look for:
- Seasonal Specials: Like springtime bamboo clams.
- Eco-Certified Stalls: Some markets label responsibly sourced seafood.

Final Bite

From smoky street stalls to upscale art-district grills, Xiamen’s seafood BBQ scene is as diverse as it is delicious. Whether you’re clinking beers at Zeng Cuo An or savoring a quiet meal in Shapowei, every bite tells a story of the sea. So grab a skewer, dig in, and let the flavors of Xiamen sweep you away.
